You open the internal door and find yourself inside a living room.
The walls are dark grey, and, if you are feeling cold, there is a fire going in the fireplace.
The mantelpiece above this has some strange objects on it, several photographs of ferrets, and a small bird inside a clock, who hoots at you and tells you the time.
A print of a frog looks down at you, very disaprovingly.

Next to the fireplace is a large shelf of board games. Interesting-looking ones.

There is a large corner sofa which has quite a lot of cushions and blankets on it. A small fox sits on the back, amongsth other creatures, on his ear is a wooden peg, it has "you look like you need a good pegging" written on it.
Steady on, you think, raising your eyebrow. It reminds you of a field in Devon, and, weirdly, you feel an overwhelming desire to find some fish. You faintly hear a song about drunken sailors (and what to do with them) playing from a mysterious source.
Bloody wizards.

There is a television, connected to a computer, who tells you that his names is Cuboid Robert (cubey Bob to his friends). You turn him on (he likes this, and whirrs appreciatively).
On the desktop you can see a shortcut for Spotify, Steam and Youtube.

There are two doors in this room, one looks like it leads into a kitchen, and the other must go into the tower that you noticed when you were outside.
